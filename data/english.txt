<h2>Hospitalization</h2>

<blockquote>
Those, who are requested to hospitalize, please fill in the request form and come to the reception desk at the 1st floor in Tochigi Children's Medical Center. Yo'll be given necessary documents.<p />
</blockquote>

Documents<br />
<blockquote>
	Confirmation Card for Hospitalization<br />
	Guide for Hospitalization<br />
	Agreement for Hospitalization<br />
	Price table for room charge<br />
</blockquote>

When the hospitalization date is defined, you'll be informed by your medical department.<p />

There're multiple room and single room. If you wish a single room, please request it at the reception desk.<p />

Within a week before the hospitalization, if the patient got an epidemic disease i.e. Cold symptoms (Snot, Cough, Fever), Rash on the body, Measles, Mumps, Chicken pox, or the patient was with such kids, please report to the reception desk.<p />

The reception desk opens from 8:30 a.m. to 5:15 p.m. on weekdays.<p />

If you change or cancel the hospitalization, please report to the reception desk.<p />

<h2>Procedure</h2>

Necessary documents<br />
<blockquote>
	Insurance Card<br />
	Your Seal<br />
	Consulation Ticket<br />
	Agreement for Hospitalization<br />
	Public expenditure certification, Limits Certification<br />
</blockquote>

Please bring them to the reception desk at the 1st floor, before you go to the room.<p />

After finishing the procedure, please come to the Staff Station at each floor.<br />
<blockquote>
	at 1:30 p.m. for 2,3,4 story room.<br />
	at 10:00 a.m. for 2B room.<br />
</blockquote>

Please show us the Insurance Card until 10th on every month.<p />

If some change occurs after the hospitalization, please bring the Insurance Card to the Staff Station or the reception desk.<p />

<h2>Life at the hospital</h2>

Daily routine<br />
<blockquote>
6:00 a.m. Wake up<br />
7:30 a.m. Breakfast<br />
10:00 a.m. Bathing (body wiping)<br />
11:30 a.m. Lunch<br />
1:00 p.m. Rest/nap<br />
3:00 p.m. Refreshment<br />
5:00 p.m. Dinner<br />
9:00 p.m. Lights-out<br />
</blockquote>
For details, please refer to 'Guide for Life at the hospital' on each floor.<p />
Nurses and caregivers do childcare.<p />
To identify the name, wrist band (with name) is attached at wrist and ankle.<p />
Identifying the name is important for the medical safety, so please cooperate with us.<p />
Please follow the instruction by doctor, nurse and staff at earthquake, disaster and emergency.<p />
No money and no articles are received during and after the hospitalization.<p />
Please consult with doctor or nurse about the treatment, the life and the care during the hospitalization.<p />
No call from outside is forwarded.<p />
